World's #1 Lasagna Recipe

Description:

A classic Italian dish filled with delicious stacked layers of Italian sausage, tomato, cheese and other assortmments of herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
