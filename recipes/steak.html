World's #1 Steak Recipe

Description:

A tried and true steak recipe that'll get you up and running with a delicious slab of steak in no time!

Ingredients:

  • Thick-cut steak - no more than 2.5cm thick, looking for boneless rib eye / scotch fillet / porterhouse / New York / T-bone.
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme

Steps:

  1. Bring the steak to room temperature before beginning.
  2. Pat the steak dry and season it generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Get the skillet SMOKING HOT before cooking.
  4. Take the steak off slightly before your preferred internal temperature, since it will rise slightly while resting.
  5. Rest for 5 minutes, allowing the meat to absorb its juices and relax its fibres.
